Common Questions

FAQs

How long does a project take? +
Most logo projects take 5–10 business days. Social media packages are delivered monthly. Web designs typically take 2–4 weeks depending on complexity. We always confirm the timeline in your proposal before starting.
Do you offer revisions? +
Yes — all Professional and Premium packages include unlimited revisions until you're completely satisfied. Starter packages include 2 rounds. We want you to love the end result.
What file formats do you deliver? +
For branding: AI, EPS, SVG, PNG, PDF (all sizes and colour variants). For print: print-ready PDF with bleeds and crop marks. For web: Figma files with assets exported as SVG/PNG/WebP. For social: high-res PNG and Canva templates.
Can I customise a package? +
Absolutely. The packages above are our most common combinations, but every project is different. Contact us with what you need and we'll build a custom quote that fits your exact requirements and budget.
How do I pay? +
We accept bank transfer, Easypaisa, JazzCash, and international payments via Payoneer or Wise. A 50% deposit is required to start, with the balance due on final delivery.
Do I own the final designs? +
Yes — upon final payment you receive full commercial ownership of all delivered files. No ongoing licensing fees, no strings attached. The designs are yours to use however you like.
Do you work with clients outside Pakistan? +
Yes! We work with clients all over the world. Communication is handled via WhatsApp, email, or video call, and we're comfortable working across time zones. Our pricing is in USD for international clients.
What if I need something urgently? +
Rush delivery is available for most projects at an additional fee. Contact us with your deadline and we'll do our best to accommodate — we've delivered complete brand identities in 48 hours when needed.
