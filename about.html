Our Story

Where it all began

Vexora Designs started in 2025 with a single laptop, two freelance clients, and a stubbornly simple idea: that every business — no matter how small — deserves design that looks expensive.

Our founder Khalid Al-Amin had spent years watching small businesses in Quetta lose customers to bigger brands, not because their products were worse, but because their visual identity didn't build trust. He knew design could change that.

What started as a one-man freelance operation quickly grew into a tight-knit team of four. Each person we brought in wasn't just talented — they were obsessive about craft. Sara obsesses over logomarks. Omar obsesses over content performance. Layla obsesses over pixel-perfect UI. That obsession is what makes the work good.

Today we work with clients across Pakistan and internationally — from local restaurants and fashion brands to tech startups and e-commerce stores. The studio is still small by choice. We believe in knowing every client by name and caring about every project personally.

What We Stand For

Our Values

✦
Craft Over Speed

We never rush a project just to hit a deadline. Every detail gets the attention it deserves — because the work represents you, not us.

◈
Honesty Always

If we think a brief needs rethinking, we'll tell you. If something won't work, we say so. Clients hire us for our expertise, not just our execution.

⬡
Small But Mighty

Being a small studio is our advantage. Every client gets a senior designer, not an intern. You'll always know who's working on your project.

▣
Results Matter

Pretty isn't enough. Everything we design has a purpose — to make people remember your brand, trust it, and choose it over the competition.

⬚
Long-Term Thinking

We design for where your business is going, not just where it is. Brand systems that grow with you and stay relevant for years.

🌍
Global Perspective

Based in Quetta, working worldwide. We bring an international design sensibility shaped by diverse cultures, markets, and visual traditions.
